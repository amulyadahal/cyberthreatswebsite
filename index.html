how to prevent computer virus and other online threats

Don't share your login credentials

One of the common mistake people make is sharing their login credientials to other people which leads to their accounts being hacked and data being stolen.

Do not use free wifi

Whenever we see free wifi espicially in cafes we immediately login to our social media and take a few snaps to post. But we might not be aware that these wifi are hotspot for hackers to lurk and steal our private data and information.

Use antivirus and firewall

Antivirus are ver much necessary to protect our devices to fall victim to malicious apps which might harm our device or even damage them.Firewall also provides some security from online hackers.

Change your password

Most of the peolpe tend to use the same login credientails especially the password in each and every account which enables the hackers to access their account easily and steal their data.So we should use strong and different passwords.

Spread awareness about such viruses

Most of the people from older and younger generations might not be aware of such cyber security threats which makes them prime target of such activities.We should educate them of such threat and how to protect themselves from it.

Frequently backup your data and reset

We should make a habit to backup our data in regular intervals so tha even if our device gets infected by malicious software we will be able to reset it without loss of data.

Steps to be taken against Computer Virus

Things you should DO

Use antivirus

Use a well renowned antivirus to protect your self online and protect your data incase of virus infection.

Perform Backup and Reset

Perform frequent backup of data and files and reset your device if necessary.

Teach people about computer virus

Most of the people from older and younger generations might not be aware of such cyber security threats which makes them prime target of such activities.We should educate them of such threat and how to protect themselves from it.

Things you should NOT DO

Download suspecious apps

We should avoid going to suspicious sites to download pirated softwares and applications as they are a hotspot for various malicious software waiting to be installed in your device and destroy it.

Share password of your device to anyone

Keep the login credientials to yourself and do not share to anyone as you do not know who might be waiting for a chance to get their hands on your device and steal your data and information.

Click on spam emails

Spam mails are very notorious for containing malicious malwares and phising sites which can only cause an individual harm.With the use of attractive looking offers and rewards which might attract the attention of unaware individual these mails are a menice to us all.
